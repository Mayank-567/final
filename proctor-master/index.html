What is Proctor Management System?

Proctor Management system contains the information about student’s personal details, faculty details and there is a mentor which monitors the group of specific students and look after the academic as well as overall progress of an individual’s.

Need Of Proctor Management System?

It will automate the manual system used for managing the sudents and faculty's information.
Students would be notified about important updates.
Students can track its own progress report.
It provides the dashboard where various kind notes, important links, previous years papers and much more is provided at single place.
